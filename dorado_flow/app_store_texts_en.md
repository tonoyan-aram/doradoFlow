## Description (up to 4000 characters)

**Chicken Cluck** is a modern app designed for creative professionals that helps you organize all your projects, ideas, and resources in one beautiful, intuitive place.

**Key Features:**

**Smart Calendar**
• Monthly calendar view with events and deadlines
• Quick access to upcoming tasks and milestones
• Visual widget showing your next important events
• Project statistics and progress tracking
• Never miss a deadline with built-in reminders

**Project Management**
• Create and organize projects of any type
• Interactive checklists with visual progress indicators
• Set deadlines and receive timely reminders
• Powerful tagging system for easy categorization
• Filter projects by type, status, or custom tags
• Track progress with beautiful progress bars

**Locations & Resources**
• Save important shooting locations for easy access
• Attach photos and detailed notes to each location
• Manage contacts associated with specific locations
• Build a comprehensive database of useful resources
• Organize locations by category and tags

**Knowledge Library**
• Curated articles and guides covering photography, video production, music creation, art, and blogging
• Practical tips and tricks for creative workflows
• Save your favorite articles for quick reference
• Powerful search functionality across all content
• Content organized by skill level (beginner, intermediate, advanced)
• Discover new techniques and inspiration

**Beautiful Modern Design**
• Sleek, modern interface with smooth animations
• Dark theme support for comfortable night-time use
• Intuitive navigation that gets out of your way
• Fully responsive design that works on all devices
• Material Design 3 principles for a familiar, polished experience

**Perfect for:**
• Photographers managing shoots and portfolios
• Videographers organizing production schedules
• Musicians tracking recording sessions and gigs
• Artists planning exhibitions and projects
• Bloggers and content creators managing content calendars
• Any creative professional who needs better organization

**Why Chicken Cluck?**
Whether you're a freelance photographer, a video production team member, a musician working on your next album, or any creative professional, **Chicken Cluck** helps you stay organized so you can focus on what you do best—creating.

Stop juggling multiple apps and sticky notes. Bring all your creative projects, deadlines, locations, and resources together in one beautifully designed app.

**Start organizing your creative workflow today!**

## Additional Tips

1. **Screenshots:** Prepare screenshots for:
   - iPhone 6.7" (iPhone 14 Pro Max, 15 Pro Max)
   - iPhone 6.5" (iPhone 11 Pro Max, XS Max)
   - iPhone 5.5" (iPhone 8 Plus)
   - iPad Pro 12.9" (if applicable)

2. **App Preview Video:** Consider creating a 15-30 second video showcasing key features

3. **App Icon:** Ensure the icon is:
   - 1024x1024 pixels
   - No transparency
   - No rounded corners (Apple will add them)
   - High quality, readable at small sizes

4. **Localization:** Consider adding these languages:
   - Spanish
   - French
   - German
   - Japanese
   - Chinese (Simplified)

5. **Update Strategy:** Plan regular updates (every 2-4 weeks) with new features and improvements

6. **ASO (App Store Optimization):**
   - Use relevant keywords naturally in description
   - Include target keywords in subtitle if possible
   - Encourage user reviews and ratings
   - Respond to user reviews promptly
