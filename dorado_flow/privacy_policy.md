# Privacy Policy for Chicken Cluck

**Last Updated:** [Date]

## Introduction

Chicken Cluck ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Chicken Cluck (the "App"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the App.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide Directly

When you use Chicken Cluck, you may voluntarily provide us with certain information, including:

- **Project Data**: Information about your projects, including titles, descriptions, deadlines, checklists, progress, tags, and notes
- **Location Data**: Location names, addresses, descriptions, coordinates (latitude/longitude), photos, contacts, and notes associated with locations
- **Event Data**: Calendar events, including titles, descriptions, dates, times, locations, and tags
- **Notes**: Personal notes, ideas, and content you create within the App
- **Task Data**: Tasks, priorities, statuses, due dates, and associated project information
- **Knowledge Library Data**: Articles you save, favorites, bookmarks, and reading preferences
- **Preferences**: App settings, theme preferences (light/dark mode), and other customization options

### Information Stored Locally

All data you create in Chicken Cluck is stored locally on your device using the device's local storage system (SharedPreferences on Android and UserDefaults/keychain on iOS). We do not have access to this data unless you explicitly share it with us.

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information**: We may collect information about your device, including device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
- **Usage Information**: We may collect information about how you interact with the App, such as features used, time spent, and error logs
- **Technical Data**: IP address, browser type, and other technical information

**Note**: Currently, Chicken Cluck does not use third-party analytics services or advertising networks. All data remains on your device.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide and Maintain the App**: To deliver the services and features you request, including project management, calendar functionality, location tracking, and knowledge library features
2. **Improve the App**: To analyze usage patterns and improve the App's functionality, user experience, and performance
3. **Communicate with You**: To respond to your inquiries, provide customer support, and send you important updates about the App
4. **Legal Compliance**: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

All data you create in Chicken Cluck is stored locally on your device. This means:
- Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export or share it
- We do not have access to your personal projects, notes, locations, or other content
- Data is stored using the device's secure local storage mechanisms
- If you delete the App, all locally stored data will be deleted

### Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:
- Data encryption in transit and at rest (when applicable)
- Secure local storage mechanisms
- Regular security assessments

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

1. **With Your Consent**: We may share your information when you explicitly consent to such sharing
2. **Service Providers**: We may share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf (e.g., cloud storage, analytics, customer support), subject to strict confidentiality obligations
3. **Legal Requirements**: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government regulation
4. **Business Transfers**: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction

**Important**: Currently, Chicken Cluck operates as a local-first application. Your data is not automatically synced to cloud services unless you explicitly enable such features in future updates.

## Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

### Access and Portability
- You can access all your data directly within the App
- You can export your data (when export features are available)

### Deletion
- You can delete individual items (projects, notes, locations, etc.) within the App
- You can delete all data by uninstalling the App
- All data stored locally will be permanently deleted when you uninstall the App

### Data Correction
- You can edit and update your data directly within the App

### Opt-Out
- Currently, we do not collect personal information for marketing purposes
- If we add features that collect additional data, we will provide opt-out mechanisms
